Text-to-Image Prompting Guide

Combine keywords to create your perfect image. The more specific, the better.

A Good Prompt Includes:

  • Subject: "A beautiful princess, a rusty robot"
  • Style: "3D render, oil painting, photo"
  • Setting: "In a magical forest, on Mars"
  • Lighting: "Cinematic lighting, soft morning light"
  • Details: "Ultra-detailed, 4K, vibrant colors"

Example Structure:

(Style) of a (Subject) in a (Setting) with (Details)

Example:

A photo of a happy dog wearing sunglasses on a beach, cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed.

Pro Tips:

  • Try adding artist names like "in the style of Van Gogh".
  • Be specific! "A red sports car" is much better than "a car".
